About us

Liftow Limited is the largest authorized Toyota forklift dealer group in North America and has been Canada's leading partner for end-to-end Toyota forklift and material handling solutions since 1960.

Our dedicated and knowledgeable service team has locations in Ontario, Quebec and The Maritimes (Liftow), as well as British Columbia (MasonLift). Our U.S. division (Northern ToyotaLift) proudly serves Vermont and surrounding area.

Our mission

Our mission is to provide our clients in the material handling industry with reliable and trusted material handling equipment, products, training, and customer service. We establish deep relationships with our clients and are committed to assisting them with finding the safest, most productive, and cost-efficient solution for their material handling needs.
